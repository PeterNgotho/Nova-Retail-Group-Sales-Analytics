# Business Questions

This project answers the following questions for Nova Retail Group leadership and store operations teams.

## Executive (CFO / CEO)

1. What is total revenue and how has it grown year-on-year?
2. Which provinces drive the most revenue, and how does revenue per store compare?
3. What is our gross margin trend by quarter?
4. Are we on track against the FY2024 revenue target of R 240M?

## Product (Buying & Merchandising)

5. Which are our top 10 products by revenue, and what share of total revenue do they represent?
6. Which categories follow the Pareto principle — i.e. which 20% of categories drive 80% of revenue?
7. How does category performance differ across provinces? (Heatmap analysis)
8. Which products have the highest return rates and lowest stock turnover?

## Store Operations

9. Which stores are top and bottom performers by revenue and basket value?
10. How does flagship vs standard vs express store performance compare?
11. Which stores have the highest revenue per square metre?

## Customer & Marketing

12. What is the revenue contribution by loyalty tier?
13. What is our repeat customer rate vs one-time buyers?
14. How does discount level affect transaction volume and total revenue?
15. Which customer age groups and provinces drive the most loyal segments?

---

Each question maps to one or more SQL queries in `sql/04_analysis_queries.sql` and to a visual on one of the Power BI dashboards.
